# Memryn Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 17, 2026

Memryn respects and protects your personal information. This Policy explains how Memryn handles data and how you can manage that data.

**Personal information handler/contact:** the Memryn developer/maintainer  
**Email:** aleancare@foxmail.com

## 1. Core Principles

Memryn is local-first. The current version does not require an account, does not provide Memryn-owned cloud storage, and does not actively collect contacts, precise location, photos, microphone, camera, advertising identifiers, or payment information. Memryn does not actively upload your notes to our own servers.

We process data only as needed for local notes, settings, workspace management, optional AI, optional long-term memory, and feedback. We try to keep data on your device or in the folder you choose.

## 2. Data We Process

**Local notes data:** Markdown files, images, attachments, whiteboard files, folder structure, file names, document links, history snapshots, and search indexes in the workspace folder you choose.

**Local configuration data:** workspace path, recently opened files, language, font size, interface state, AI provider, model, endpoint, API key, long-term memory settings, index state, and app preferences.

**AI interaction data:** when you actively use AI, this may include your prompt, selected text, current-note snippets, retrieved related-note snippets, context, model parameters, and AI responses.

**Feedback data:** if you email us, this may include your email address, message content, screenshots, logs, system information, or issue descriptions that you choose to provide.

## 3. Purposes and Methods

Memryn reads and writes the workspace you choose on your device to show the file tree, open and save files, render preview, create history snapshots, build local search indexes, maintain document links, and perform edits you trigger.

Memryn stores local configuration to remember language, font size, workspace, AI keys, and other preferences. API keys are used only for requests to the provider you configured and are not actively sent to Memryn-owned servers.

When you actively use AI features, Memryn sends data needed for that request to the third-party AI or embeddings provider you selected. You can choose not to configure AI and can keep long-term memory off. Normal local writing does not require AI.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Cross-Border Processing

If you choose OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Gemini, an OpenAI-compatible gateway, a company proxy, or another third-party model service, relevant data is sent to the service endpoint you configured. That provider may be located inside or outside China and processes data under its own terms, privacy policy, account settings, and applicable law.

If you place your notes folder in iCloud Drive, a cloud drive, NAS, enterprise sync folder, or other sync location, file sync, cloud backup, download/removal of local copies, conflict handling, and data location are controlled by that third party. Memryn cannot control those third-party practices.

## 5. Retention

Local notes, history snapshots, whiteboards, images, attachments, indexes, and settings remain on your device or in your chosen workspace until you delete them, clean them, move the workspace, uninstall the app, or the system/sync service removes them.

Retention after AI requests are sent to a third party is governed by that provider's policy.

Feedback emails are retained for a reasonable period needed to handle the issue, communicate with you, and keep necessary records.

## 6. Your Controls and Rights

You can view, copy, edit, move, or delete files in your workspace at any time.

You can clear AI keys, turn off long-term memory, switch providers, or change workspace in settings.

You can delete the `.memoir` folder, history snapshots, index files, or related configuration in the workspace.

Clearing the workspace only makes Memryn leave the current folder; it does not delete your note files.

For requests to access, correct, delete, withdraw consent, obtain explanations, or complain about personal information handling, contact aleancare@foxmail.com.

Because Memryn mainly processes data locally on your device, we may need you to identify the device, version, workspace location, or data type so we can determine how to help.

## 7. Security and Risk Notice

Memryn tries to minimize unnecessary data transfers and stores workspace files and settings locally.

Local file security depends on your device, OS account, disk, backups, sync services, third-party services, and access permissions.

Please protect your computer password, disk encryption, iCloud/cloud-drive accounts, API keys, and sensitive notes.

AI, long-term memory, and third-party sync are not suitable by default for highly sensitive, classified, regulated, or organization-restricted information.

Before processing company material, customer data, student data, medical, financial, HR, trade secret, or other sensitive content, check your authority and organizational rules.

## 8. Minors

Memryn is intended for users with appropriate legal capacity.

Minors should use Memryn only with guardian consent and guidance.

Do not process minors' sensitive personal information through Memryn without proper authorization.

## 9. Updates

We may update this Policy to reflect product changes, third-party service changes, or legal requirements.

Material changes will be presented in the product in an appropriate way.

Continued use of Memryn means you accept the updated Policy.

## 10. Contact

For questions, suggestions, complaints, or rights requests about this Policy or data processing, contact us through the feedback email in Settings or at aleancare@foxmail.com.
